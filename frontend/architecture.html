Layer 2 — Cross-Validation Engine (10 Checks)

Rule-based validation layer. Compares extracted figures against banking thresholds. Each check yields a severity level and a PASS/FAIL/FLAG result.

CV_001 — Interest Coverage Ratio

ICR = EBITDA / Interest Expense
Condition Result Severity
ICR ≥ 1.5x PASS LOW
ICR < 1.5x FAIL HIGH
📌 RBI mandates ICR > 1.5x for term loans. ICR < 1.0x means the company cannot cover interest from operating profits.

CV_002 — Gearing Ratio (Debt/Equity)

Gearing = Total Debt / Shareholders' Equity
Condition Result Severity
Gearing < 1.0x PASS LOW
1.0x ≤ Gearing < 2.0x WARN MEDIUM
Gearing ≥ 2.0x FAIL CRITICAL

CV_003 — PAT Margin

PAT Margin = (Profit After Tax / Revenue) × 100
Condition Result Severity
PAT Margin ≥ 2% PASS LOW
PAT Margin < 2% WARN MEDIUM

CV_004 — Debt Trend

Debt Trend% = ((Debtlatest − Debtearliest) / Debtearliest) × 100
Condition Result Severity
Debt reduced > 10% PASS LOW
Stable (±10%) PASS LOW
Grew 10–30% WATCH MEDIUM
Grew > 30% WARN HIGH

CV_005 — Cash Trend

Cash Trend% = ((Cashlatest − Cashearliest) / Cashearliest) × 100
Condition Result Severity
Cash grew or stable PASS LOW
Declined 10–30% WATCH MEDIUM
Declined > 30% WARN — Liquidity Stress HIGH

CV_006 — Revenue Consistency

Revenue Growth% = ((Revlatest − Revearliest) / Revearliest) × 100
Condition Result Severity
-10% ≤ Δ ≤ 100% PASS — Normal LOW
Δ > 100% FLAG — Verify vs GST/bank HIGH
Δ < -30% WARN — Investigate HIGH
⚠️ India-Specific: Revenue growth > 100% in 2 years without corresponding GST/bank data is a classic circular trading signal.

CV_007 — Audit Qualifications

IF audit_qualifications[] is NOT empty → FLAG (HIGH)

CV_008 — Litigation Detection

Keyword scan: ["litigation", "court", "ibc", "nclt", "insolvency", "cheque bounce"]

CV_009 — GST vs Bank Statement (Circular Trading Detector) ⚠️

Ratio = GST_Turnover / Bank_Credits
Mismatch% = (Ratio − 1) × 100
Condition Result Severity
Ratio ≤ 1.1x PASS LOW
1.1x < Ratio ≤ 1.3x WARN — Minor Mismatch MEDIUM
Ratio > 1.3x CRITICAL — Circular Trading CRITICAL
⚠️ This is the most powerful fraud detection check. If GST turnover exceeds bank credits by >30%, someone is generating fake invoices.

CV_010 — ITC Ratio vs Sector Benchmark

Actual ITC Ratio = ITC_Claimed / GST_Turnover
Deviation% = ((Actual − Benchmark) / Benchmark) × 100

Layer 3 — Risk Scoring Engine

Each dimension is scored from 0 (safest) to 10 (riskiest).

Individual Scores

Financial Risk Score (fallback) = min(Critical × 3 + High × 2 + Medium, 10)

Overall Credit Score

Overall = (Financial + Compliance + Management + Industry) / 4

Risk Category Mapping

Score Category Interpretation
0.0 — 3.0 Low Strong credit profile, minimal risk
3.1 — 5.0 Moderate Acceptable with monitoring
5.1 — 7.0 High Significant risk, enhanced due diligence required
7.1 — 10.0 Very High Potential NPA, decline or restructure
